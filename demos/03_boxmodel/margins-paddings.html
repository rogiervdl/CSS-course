Margins & paddings behaviour

Note: all margins & paddings are set to 20px in this demo
Note: use your chrome inspector to see the margins

1. Blocklevel, unpositioned: vertical margins "collapse" (i.e. the greatest value of touching margins counts)

2. Blocklevel, positioned: horizontal & vertical margins add up

3. Inline: vertical margins are ignored, horizontal margins add up
